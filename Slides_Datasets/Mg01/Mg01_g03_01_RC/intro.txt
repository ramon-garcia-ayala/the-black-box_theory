Let's take a look at the Robot Garden

an outdoor garden at the University of Michigan's Robotics building, inaugurated in 2021

It is revolutionary because the design of the ground and the textures of the garden were not 
designed by an architect sitting with a mouse. 

What they did was take satellite images of the terrain, train a neural network with thousands 
of images of architectural elements 

and then let the network dream those elements onto the image of the site.
