INTERNAL RELATIONS

The black box is not just hidden.
It is organized.

For Simondon, what matters is not that we cannot see inside —
it is that the inside has a structure.

An ensemble of functional relations
that produce the behavior we observe.

In the AI model:
parameters and weights
trained patterns
latent dimensional relations
encoded aesthetic tendencies

These are not secrets.
They are relations —
and relations can be read.

Opening the box means reading those relations.
Not just the output they produce.
Not just the names of the pipeline stages.
